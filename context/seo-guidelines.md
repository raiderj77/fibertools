# fibertools.app — SEO Guidelines

## Primary Content Focus
Fiber arts how-to, knitting patterns guidance, crochet tutorials, yarn selection, tool use, project planning.

## Keyword Strategy
- Target: "how to [fiber arts task]", "yarn weight for [project]", "knitting vs crochet [comparison]"
- Opportunity: long-tail pattern-adjacent content (avoid competing on exact pattern keywords)
- Focus on: calculator and tool use cases (strong tie-in to our tools)

## Internal Linking
Always link to the relevant tool when discussing a calculation or measurement task.

## Meta Title Format
`[Topic] | Free Fiber Arts Tools | fibertools.app`

## Schema
Use HowTo schema for tutorials. Use FAQPage for FAQ sections. Use Article schema.

## GEO Requirements
- 40–60 word direct answer blocks under every H2
- Fiber arts definitions are highly citable by AI — always define terms clearly
