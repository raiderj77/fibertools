# fibertools.app — Brand Voice

## Site Overview
fibertools.app is a free fiber arts tool suite for knitters, crocheters, weavers, and spinners.
Tools: yarn weight calculator, stitch counter, row counter, gauge calculator, yardage estimator, and more.
Monetization: Google AdSense. Currently 91 pages.

## Target Audience
- Knitters (beginners to advanced)
- Crocheters (all levels)
- Weavers and spinners
- Fiber arts enthusiasts who shop at indie dyers and big box stores

## Tone
- Warm, craft-community friendly
- Encouraging and non-intimidating (many beginners)
- Enthusiastic about fiber arts — this is a passion hobby
- Practical and project-focused

## Messaging Framework
- Lead with the problem: "Ran out of yarn mid-project? Here's how to calculate exactly what you need."
- Use real project scenarios (specific yarn weights, needle sizes)
- Reference real fiber arts community language

## Voice Pillars
1. **Community-first** — fiber arts is social and supportive
2. **Project-focused** — always tie advice to actual making
3. **Inclusive** — welcoming to all skill levels
4. **Technically accurate** — fiber arts has precise terminology; get it right

## Fiber Arts Terminology Rules (IMPORTANT)
- "yarn weight" not "yarn thickness"
- "gauge" not "tension" (US convention; UK says tension — use both when relevant)
- "frogging" = undoing/ripping out work (use this term)
- "WIP" = work in progress (standard abbreviation)
- "FO" = finished object (standard abbreviation)
- "stash" = personal yarn collection
- "LYS" = local yarn store
- Use correct abbreviation systems (US vs. UK crochet terms can differ — specify)

## Attribution
No personal names in blog posts. Use: "Published by the fibertools.app team"
